 Sales Performance & Customer Insights Dashboard (Power BI)
 Project Overview

This project demonstrates an end-to-end data analysis and visualization workflow using Excel and Power BI.
The goal of the project is to analyze sales performance, understand customer behavior, and provide actionable business insights through interactive dashboards.

The project covers:

Data cleaning and modeling

Measure creation using DAX

Interactive dashboards with slicers

Business insights and storytelling

Tools & Technologies Used

Microsoft Excel – Data source

Power BI Desktop – Data modeling, DAX, visualization

Power Query – Data transformation

DAX – Measures and calculations

 Dataset Description

The dataset contains simulated sales data with the following key attributes:

Order Date

Customer Details (Name, Gender, Age Group)

Region, State, City

Product Category & Sub-Category

Sales Channel

Payment Mode

Quantity, Unit Price, Discount, Net Sales

 Dashboards Included
 Sales Performance Analysis

Insights covered:

Total Sales by Region

Sales by Product Category

Monthly Sales Trend

Key Learnings:

Identified top-performing regions and categories

Analyzed seasonal sales trends

 Customer Insights Dashboard

Insights covered:

Sales contribution by Gender

Sales by Age Group

Top 5 Customers by Sales

Sales distribution across cities

Key Learnings:

Understanding customer demographics

Identifying high-value customers

 Interactive Dashboard (Slicers)

Slicers added:

Region

Category

Payment Mode

Sales Channel

All visuals update dynamically based on slicer selection, enabling interactive analysis.

 Insights & Storytelling

This page summarizes business insights and recommendations, including:

High-performing regions and categories

Customer spending behavior

Preferred payment modes and sales channels

Actionable recommendations for marketing and customer retention

 Key Business Insights

The South region contributes the highest share of total sales.

Electronics is the highest revenue-generating category.

Customers aged 26–35 are the highest spenders.

Digital payment modes (UPI / Cards) dominate transactions.

Online sales channels outperform offline channels.
